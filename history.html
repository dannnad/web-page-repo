History

Established in 2010 by visionary entrepreneur, Alexander Brooks, Brocadero emerged as a small start-up in the heart of Silicon Valley. Originally focusing on innovative smart devices, the company quickly gained recognition for its seamless blend of cutting-edge technology and elegant design. Over the years, Brocadero's relentless commitment to pushing boundaries and delivering excellence propelled it to global prominence. Evolving beyond tech, they expanded into the realm of luxury goods, creating a unique brand that embodies sophistication and innovation. Today, Brocadero stands as a beacon of modern living, elevating lifestyles worldwide.
